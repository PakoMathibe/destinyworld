ABOUT US

Destiny World

Destiny World was founded with the idea of making a recognizable contribution in the mining and public sectors by offering reliable, efficient, fast and quality rope access service. We make it our priority to understand the needs of our clients and provide them with services and products which help them achieve their objectives. Our company philosophy is focused on being excellent in our service delivery and customer satisfaction. We don’t compromise on the quality of the services and products we supply.

OUR VISION

Be a leader in rope access techniques and mining safety solutions.

OUR MISSION

Build a company where the environment is condusive to growth for employees.

OUR VALUES 

Provide excellent services to our clients and partners, insuring that we maintain a long-term caring and successful relationship
